Abstract

This project aims to develop a real-time flood monitoring system using IoT technologies such as ESP32, ultrasonic sensors, GSM modules, and cloud-based data visualization through ThingSpeak. The system is designed to provide timely alerts via SMS and web-based dashboards to help residents and local authorities prepare for rising water levels. The integration of prediction models and historical data analysis enhances the accuracy and reliability of flood detection, ultimately contributing to disaster preparedness and community safety.
